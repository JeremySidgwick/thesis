Help page

Navigation on the image

To navigate around the image, use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
To move: hold down the left mouse button outside a rectangle.

How to make the transcription

  1. Create your first rectangle by clicking on the ‘add new rectangle’ button at the bottom left.
  2. Adjust its position, size and inclination to match the line of text as closely as possible.
  3. Once the first rectangle is correctly positioned, duplicate it by clicking on the icon at the top left of the rectangle.
  4. Adjust the rectangle and repeat the process for each line.
  5. Once the lines have been labelled, click on the ‘save rectangles’ button at the bottom left of the page.
  6. Transcribe each line into the corresponding text box on the right-hand side of the page.
  7. Click on the ‘save progress’ button to save the progress or on ‘submit to verification’ to complete the transcription.

How to make the verification

For verification, the verifier can take four actions for each rectangle:

  • Do nothing: this means that the transcription is accepted. The rectangle will be validated and can no longer be modified.
  • Delete the rectangle: by clicking on the bin icon, the rectangle can be permanently deleted if it does not correspond to a line.
  • Modify the text: by clicking on the pencil icon, the checker can suggest another transcription. This step will involve an additional verification step.
  • Access the forum: by clicking on the blue icon, the user can access or create a forum linked to this rectangle or document.

The checker can save their progress, complete the check to finish the task, or send the document back to the transcription stage if the work produced is not satisfactory.
